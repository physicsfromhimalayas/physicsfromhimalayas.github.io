What Do We Offer

Here are some events and opportunities which we offer to our students and members.

Conferences and Webinars

We organize conferences and webinars featuring renowned scientists and experts in the field of physics.

PLANCKS

We participate in PLANCKS, an international competition on Theoretical physics.

Physics Olympiad

We provide an opportunity for students to showcase their skills and knowledge in physics and compete with fellow students from across the country.

Elocution Contest

We conduct Elocution Contests where students get to showcase their public speaking skills and share their ideas on physics-related topics.
